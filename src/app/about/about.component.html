About Us

The Founders of TME-Systems and a Core Group of Change Advocates have spent more than Two Decades perfecting a Social Enterprise Business Model that Directly Addresses Social and Economic Inequality Specific to Disenfranchised Communities. What originally started out as an idea to help Improve the Economic Conditions of the Disenfranchised, has now morphed into a Full Fledged Social Enterprise Business Module. TME-Systems Does NOT conduct Business as Usual! Make no mistake! Our Directive is Crystal Clear. When Developing Eco-Systems, our Highest Priority has always been to Ensure those Systems Produce Immediate Social/Economic Impact, Create an Environment of Equality and Sets a Higher Standard for Inclusion and Equal Access for All.

Force Change

The power to force Social and Economic Change rests Squarely on the Shoulders of the people who Desperately Need Change to happen Right Now! The Disenfranchised Possess That Power! The question of Available Resources is Not the Issue. The Resources have Always Been There! The Question is the Willingness of those who have been Impacted or Oppressed, to Stand Up and be Counted among this New Wave of Leadership! There are certainly More Than Enough people living in Disenfranchised Situations to Work Together and Harness the Power They Do Have to Help their own Social/Economic Circumstances!

Action Based

In General, what is missing is Equal Access to a National Plan of Action, Coordinated at the Local Level, to Consolidate the Micro Economic Power of the Disenfranchised. The National Task Force (NTF) was created in part, to Increase the Awareness and the Economic Potential of that Power! As True Believers in Equality our Organization Specializes in Identifying Existing Resources within a Community. (without exception, every community has them) By Design, It Does Not Matter if the Resources are Large or Small. The NTF Consolidates and Redirects those Resources Back into the Community to Create a Systematic Flow of Social and Economic Benefits from the Bottom Up! Increasing Personal and Household Income, Community Based Revenue Development, Small Business Infrastructure, Job Creation and much more, at the Grassroots Level! We refer to this as Trickle Up Economics!

Revenue Development Opportunities for Individuals, Organizations and Businesses are all part of our Common Sense Solutions Action Plan.
